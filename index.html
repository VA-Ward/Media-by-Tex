Meet Tex / 01

Gunditjmara man,
behind the lens.

Tex Walker is a photographer living and working amongst the Kulin Nations. A proud Gunditjmara man, he points the camera at the things he cares about — the land, the people on it, and the brands and athletes shaping culture today.

His work moves between commercial shoots and quiet frames of Country. Whether it's a brand campaign, an athlete mid-stride, or first light over the land, the aim stays the same: honest images with a real sense of place.

"Working with brands, athletes and taking photos of Country."— Tex

Open to commissions across Victoria and beyond.
